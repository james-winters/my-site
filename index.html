Welcome!

I’m so glad you’re here.

This portfolio showcases my work at the intersection of instructional design, chemical education, and inclusive learning. From developing Course-based Undergraduate Research Experiences (CUREs) to designing scaffolded video tutorials and educational games, each artifact reflects my passion for turning complex ideas into accessible, engaging learning experiences.

My goal is to create learning environments that are thoughtful, rigorous, and fun. I hope this portfolio offers a meaningful glimpse into the instructional values and vision I bring to every learning space.

As you explore, you will find examples of:
• Student-centered curriculum design rooted in Universal Design for Learning and ADDIE frameworks
• General Chemistry II Laboratory Experiments that show unique visualization of reactions
• Chemistry educational games that meld science and fun
• Mentorship-focused training that empowers educators and learners
