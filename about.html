James R. Winters, Ph.D.

Pronouns: he/him/his
Email Me

A meticulous and organized Postdoctoral Associate working in Chemistry education research to enhance General Chemistry for better student outcomes. Experienced in collaborating with faculty, staff, teaching assistants, graduate and undergraduate students, and industry researchers. Proficient in coordinating multiple projects simultaneously as well as managing the personnel on said projects. Created a General Chemistry laboratory course curriculum, while developing a research plan to assess the effectiveness of the course to increase students’ laboratory skills. Co-created a gamified General Chemistry II lecture for STEM Nonmajors which utilizes hybrid specifications-based/ experience points grading scheme for a leveling up system. The course is fantasy themed and includes 5 digital escape rooms as exam review activities.
