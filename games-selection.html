Le Châtelier’s Principle Adventure Map

The player is on a quest through a fantasy land. They are given a map and told that at each fork in the road there is a sign with a reaction and something that happened to the reaction. The player is to go the direction that the Le Châtelier’s Principle would shift the reaction until they find the quest item.

Determining a Weak Acid Concentration

This activity is designed to support the instruction of weak acid titrations. The goal is to determine the amount of acetic acid in a vinegar samples. The players begin with familiarizing themselves with a weak acid titration by using a known concentration of acetic acid. They then use that general knowledge to 'titrate' the unknown sample and determine its acetic acid concentration.
